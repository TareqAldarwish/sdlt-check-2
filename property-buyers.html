What is stamp duty?

Stamp Duty is a tax you might have to pay if you buy a residential property or a piece of land in England or Northern Ireland over a certain price. You conveyancer will need to work out how much, if any Stamp Duty you are required to pay as part of the conveyancing process.

This tax applies to both freehold and leasehold properties - whether you're buying outright or with a mortgage.

If you'rs buying a property in England you will pay SDLT (Stamp Duty Land Tax), in Scotland, you will pay Land and Buildings Transaction Tax (LBTT) and in Wales, Land Transaction Tax (LTT).

Using SDLT Check to pay the right Stamp Duty
